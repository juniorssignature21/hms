Summary of Accounting Types

    Revenue Accounting:
        Room, Event, and Service Revenue
        Deferred Revenue
        POS Sales Revenue

    Expense Accounting:
        Payroll and Benefits
        Inventory and COGS
        Procurement and Operating Expenses

    Accounts Receivable and Accounts Payable:
        Customer Invoices and Supplier Payments
        Credit and Prepayments

    Tax Accounting:
        Sales Tax, Payroll Tax, and Occupancy Tax

    Inventory and Asset Accounting:
        Inventory Valuation
        Fixed Asset Depreciation

    Financial Reporting:
        Income Statements
        Balance Sheets
        Cash Flow Statements
